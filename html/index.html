Instantly Create Site Preview Links

PreviewSites.dev offers a seamless preview site solution for websites during server migrations. No more /etc/hosts modifications needed - simply enter your domain and new IP address to generate an instant site preview link. Our preview site tool streamlines the migration verification process, making it the perfect alternative to traditional /etc/hosts editing. Ideal for developers and teams who need quick, secure website previews before DNS changes.

How to Use PreviewSites.dev

Easily generate a site preview after migration to verify your website before final deployment.

Step 1: Enter Your Domain & New IP

Go to PreviewSites.dev and input your domain and the new server IP. This will generate a preview site link.

Step 2: Click "Generate Preview"

Click the "Submit" button, and within seconds, you will receive a temporary tools site preview link.

Step 3: Verify & Test Your Site

Use the provided site preview migration link to test your website. The preview link is active for 3 hours.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PreviewSites.dev.

➤ What is PreviewSites.dev?
PreviewSites.dev is a tool that generates temporary preview links for websites after migration, allowing you and your clients to test without changing local configurations.
➤ How long does a preview link remain active?
Preview links are active for 3 hours and are automatically removed after expiration.
➤ Do I need to modify my /etc/hosts file?
No, you can generate a preview link and share it directly without modifying your local /etc/hosts file.
➤ Are the preview links secure?
Yes, all preview links are SSL-secured, ensuring encrypted and safe connections.
➤ Who can access the preview link?
Anyone with the generated preview link can access the temporary preview site.
➤ Is PreviewSites.dev open-source?
Yes, PreviewSites.dev is an open-source project designed to help developers test migrations seamlessly.
